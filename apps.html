Laboratory Apps for
Android & iOS

Take your lab tools off the bench and into your pocket. All apps are free, built from real laboratory workflows, and work offline — no account or internet connection required.

Android 8.0+ iOS 14+ Works offline No account needed
App 01 / 03
LabCalc

Your complete reagent calculation toolkit — molarity, dilutions, percent solutions, unit conversions, and buffer preparation, all in one offline app.

  • Molarity calculator with built-in molar mass database (500+ compounds)
  • C₁V₁ = C₂V₂ dilution solver — find any unknown in seconds
  • % w/v, % w/w, % v/v percent solution calculator
  • Concentration unit converter across 11 units (mol/L, mg/dL, ppm, and more)
  • Buffer preparation guide with Henderson-Hasselbalch calculator
  • Calculation history — save and revisit previous results
  • Fully offline — no internet connection required
v1.0.0 · ~8 MB · Last updated Jan 2025
App 02 / 03
LabStock

Chemical inventory management designed for the real lab bench — track reagents, get low-stock alerts, manage expiry dates, and generate usage reports.

  • Barcode and QR code scanning to log chemicals in seconds
  • Low-stock threshold alerts with push notifications
  • Expiry date tracking — see at a glance what is expiring soon
  • Location tagging — record which shelf, fridge, or cabinet
  • Usage log — record withdrawals with date, user, and quantity
  • Export inventory as CSV or PDF for audits and orders
  • GHS hazard class display alongside each chemical record
v1.0.0 · ~11 MB · Last updated Jan 2025
App 03 / 03
LabLog

Digital instrument logbook and maintenance tracker — record usage, log calibrations, schedule servicing, and keep your instruments audit-ready at all times.

  • Instrument registry — add any instrument with model, serial, and location
  • Digital run log — record every use with operator, date, sample count, and notes
  • Calibration records with pass/fail status and certificate attachment
  • Maintenance scheduler — set recurring service intervals with reminders
  • Fault / incident reporting with photo attachment
  • PDF export of full instrument history for audits and accreditation
  • Multi-instrument dashboard — see all equipment status at a glance
v1.0.0 · ~9 MB · Last updated Jan 2025
